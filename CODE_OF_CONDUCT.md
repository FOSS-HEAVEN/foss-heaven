# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This is a small project. We don't need a 5-page legal document. Just:

- **Be honest.** About tools. About your experience. About what you don't know.
- **Be respectful.** Even when you disagree. Especially when you disagree.
- **Be constructive.** "This sucks" is fine if you explain why. "This is garbage" without context is not.
- **Be patient.** Maintainers have jobs. Reviews take time.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment of any kind
- Spam or promotional content
- Demanding updates on a schedule
- Arguing in bad faith
- Doxxing or privacy violations

## Enforcement

We reserve the right to:
- Close issues that violate these standards
- Reject PRs that don't meet quality bars
- Block users who are consistently disruptive

No warnings. No appeals process. This is not a government. It's a GitHub repo.

## Reporting

If you see something, say something. Open an issue or email the maintainers directly.
